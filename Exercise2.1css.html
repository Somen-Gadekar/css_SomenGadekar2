Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • Extra virgin olive oil.
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated.
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps.
  • Fontina cheese, grated.
  • Parmesan cheese, grated.
  • Feta cheese, crumbled.

    • Steps to Make Pizza from Scratch:

    1. Prepare Dough:Mix Ingredients: Combine flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt.Knead: Work the dough until smooth, then let it rise. 🍞👩‍🍳
    2. Roll Out Dough:Flour Surface: Roll the dough into desired thickness.Shape: Create a round or rectangular base. 🍕🔍
    3. Add Sauce and Toppings:Spread Sauce: Tomato sauce or olive oil for the base.Cheese and More: Layer with mozzarella and favorite toppings. 🍅🧀
    4. Bake:Preheat Oven: Ensure it's hot (around 475°F or 245°C).Cook Time: Bake until crust is golden and cheese melts (12-15 minutes). 🌡️🕒
    5. Enjoy Fresh:Slice and Serve: Let it cool slightly before cutting.Garnish: Fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil for a finishing touch. 🍽️🌿
